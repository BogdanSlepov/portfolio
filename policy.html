Policy regarding the processing of personal data

1. General Provisions

1.1. This Personal Data Processing Policy (hereinafter referred to as the Policy) was developed in accordance with the Law of Ukraine dated 01.06.2010 No. 2297-VI "On the Protection of Personal Data" (hereinafter referred to as the Law) and the legislation of the European Union in the field of personal data protection of the subjects of the European Union, in cases of processing such data, and is an internal regulatory document of the Development (hereinafter referred to as the Developer), which determines the key areas of its activities in the field of processing and protecting personal data. 1.2. The policy was developed in order to implement the requirements of the current legislation in the field of protection and processing of personal data and is aimed at protecting the fundamental rights and freedoms of a person and citizen in cases where the Developer processes his personal data. 2. Purposes and basic conditions for the processing of personal data 2.1. The processing of personal data is carried out by the Developer in order to: • conclusion and execution of contracts; • notification of users (including potential) of the Developer's services about the services, provided discounts, promotions and other events of the Developer, about changes in the services and work of the Developer; • conducting surveys to improve the quality of service; • improving the quality of user service (including potential) services Developer; • implementation of additional incentive programs for service users; • training and career development of employees, taking into account the results of employees' performance of their duties, providing the employee with working conditions, guarantees and compensations established by the legislation of Ukraine, ensuring the fulfillment of contractual agreements with employees, fulfillment of social obligations to employees; • for other purposes stipulated by the internal regulatory documents of the Developer and the current legislation of Ukraine. 2.2. To achieve the goals specified in clause 2.1. Policies, personal data may be processed within the periods established by the legislation of Ukraine for the storage of relevant documentation (medical, documentation in the field of work, etc.), and if such periods are not established, within 5 years. 2.3. The processing of personal data in the Clinic is carried out in compliance with the requirements provided for by the Law, namely: • the purpose of processing personal data must be formulated in laws, other legal acts, regulations, constituent or other documents regulating the activities of the owner of the personal data base, and comply with the legislation of Ukraine on the protection of personal data; • personal data must be accurate, reliable and, if necessary, updated; • the composition and content of personal data must be appropriate, but not excessive in relation to the specific purpose of their processing; • the amount of personal data that can be included in the personal data base is determined by the terms of the consent of the subject of personal data or in accordance with the law; • the processing of personal data is carried out for specific and legitimate purposes, determined by the consent of the subject of personal data or in cases provided for by the laws of Ukraine, in the manner prescribed by law; • it is not allowed to process data about an individual without his consent, except in cases specified by law, and only in the interests of national security, economic well-being and human rights; • if the processing of personal data is necessary to protect the vital interests of the subject of personal data, it is possible to process personal data without his consent until the moment when obtaining consent becomes possible; • personal data are processed in a form that allows the identification of the natural person to whom they concern, no longer than is necessary for the legitimate purposes for which they were collected or further processed. 2.4. The processing of personal data includes the collection, recording, systematization, accumulation, storage, clarification (updating, changing), extraction, use, distribution (including transfer to the territory of Ukraine and cross-border transfer), depersonalization, blocking, destruction of personal data, as well as transfer to their counterparties Clinics. 2.5. The processing of personal data can be carried out using computer technology (automated processing) or with the direct participation of a person without the use of computer technology (manual processing). 2.6. When processing personal data, the Clinic provides the necessary conditions for the unhindered exercise by the subject of personal data of their personal non-property rights to their personal data. The disposal of personal data of an individual, limited in civil capacity or recognized as legally incompetent, is carried out by his legal representative. 2.7.
